Hyde Park

Check it out!

  • For three glorious days, we're taking over the entirety of Hyde Park. Located in the heart of London, Hyde Park is 350 acres in total.
  • In between the festivities, be sure to check out these "can't miss" features and landmarks

The Serpentine

Need a break from felines? Not to worry, we won't judge! Take a paddle or row boat for a spin around Hyde Park's famous Serpentine. Be on the look out for swans, ducks, and other local water fowel. For more information on prices and hours, please check out the Royal Parks website

Hyde Park Bandstand

Our handpicked vendors will be around the band stand selling toys, furniture, and other specialty goods for your feline companions.

Keynote Speaker: Jackson Galaxy

Now You're Talking My Langage: Communicating with Your Cat

Jackson Galaxy is a cat behaviorist and the host of the television show My Cat from Hell. Working one-on-one with cats in their homes, he works with clients to improve their cats' behavioral issues. Galaxy also works closely with animal shelters and rescue organizations, teaching his "Cat Mojo" approach to feline behavior to volunteers, staff and adopters and helping with both behavioral and environmental enrichment programs for their feline residents. As our keynote speaker, Jackson Galaxy will talk to us about his "catification" process to help your cat feel more comfortable in your home and how to communicate with your cat. Jodie Mancy

Working Cats: The Backbone of the World

Cats might sleep the vast majority of the lives. And yes, they can barely reach their cat toys sometimes. Some cats, though, are working hard for their kibble. Join Jodie as she discusses cats that work hard and nap hard. From barn cats who keep mice out, to greeters at your local supermarket, to emotional support cats who help patients undergo intensive hospital treatment, cats are more varsitile than you think.

Sophie Dorncaster

From Drab to Fab: Grooming your Lion 101

Ensuring your fabulous feline is looking good inside and out is key to your furry friend's hapiness. Sophie has been consulting with groomers and cat owners accross the globe for over 15 years. She has revolutionised the grooming industry by coming up with cat-friendly techniques to groom. In this talk, you'll learn the best methods to groom your cat, how to help them enjoy grooming, and top tips on making your cat look their best in photos.
